About TumorDetect AI

Project Overview

TumorDetect AI is an advanced diagnostic application that analyzes MRI scans to identify and classify brain tumors. By leveraging deep learning and computer vision technologies, our system provides radiologists with accurate insights and reduces interpretation time.

Technology

Our system uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) built with TensorFlow to analyze brain MRI images. The model is trained on a diverse dataset of brain MRI scans and can classify tumors into different categories:

  • Glioma: A type of tumor that occurs in the brain and spinal cord.
  • Meningioma: A tumor that forms on membranes that cover the brain and spinal cord inside the skull.
  • Pituitary Tumor: A growth that develops in the pituitary gland at the base of the brain.
  • No Tumor: Normal brain tissue without any tumor present.

Benefits

Enhanced Accuracy

Our AI model achieves high accuracy in tumor detection and classification, providing reliable second opinions.

Time Efficiency

Reduces the time required for diagnosis, allowing radiologists to focus on complex cases.

Accessibility

Makes advanced diagnostic capabilities available to medical facilities with limited specialist access.

Continuous Learning

Our model improves over time as it processes more images and receives feedback.
